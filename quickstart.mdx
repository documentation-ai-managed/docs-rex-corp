## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An active internet connection
- A payment method ready for ticket purchases (credit card or digital wallet)
</Callout>

## Quick Navigation Steps

Follow these steps to get up and running with the Usupovo Life Hall website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Homepage" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://www.usupovolifehall.com`.

    The homepage features a hero banner with featured events and a prominent search bar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the Event Calendar" icon="calendar">
    Click the `Events` tab in the top navigation menu.

    You'll see a monthly calendar view highlighting upcoming performances like "Jazz Nights on Fridays" or "Symphony Orchestra Gala".
    
    Use the filters for genre (e.g., rock, classical) or date range to narrow results.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for Specific Performances" icon="search">
    Enter keywords in the search bar, such as `rock concert` or `artist name`.

    Results display event details including date, time, venue capacity (`>500 seats`), and ticket availability.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete a Ticket Purchase" icon="shopping-cart">
    Select an event and click `Buy Tickets`.

    Choose seats from the interactive venue map, add to cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your details and payment info securely.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Venue Information" icon="map">
    From the footer or top menu, select `Venue` to view layout diagrams, parking details, and accessibility info.

    Download the PDF seat map for reference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform-Specific Tips

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop" icon="monitor">
    Use keyboard shortcuts like `Ctrl+F` to search the page quickly. The calendar supports mouse hover for event previews.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile" icon="smartphone">
    Tap the hamburger menu (three lines) for navigation. Pinch to zoom on the seat map during purchase.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Tablet" icon="tablet">
    Landscape mode optimizes the event grid. Enable notifications for sold-out alerts.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
