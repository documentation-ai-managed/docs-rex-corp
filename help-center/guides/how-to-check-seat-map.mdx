## Seat Map Guide

See where your seats are before booking!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Event">
    Go to `https://usupovo.rexcorp.com/events` and select the event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Seat Map">
    Click "View Seat Map" on the event page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and Choose">
    Hover over seats for pricing and view. Select your desired section before checkout.
  </Step>
</Steps>
