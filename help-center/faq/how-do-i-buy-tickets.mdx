description: Guide to purchasing tickets for Usupovo Life Hall events.

## Overview

All public events list ticket options on the Usupovo Life Hall website.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://usupovo.rexcorp.com/events`
2. Select your event
3. Click "Buy Tickets"
4. Choose seats and quantity
5. Enter payment details and confirm

Your e-tickets are emailed to you after purchase.
