---
title: Ticket Not Received
description: What to do if you haven't received your e-tickets.
---

## Symptoms

- Bought tickets but no email received
- Can't find e-mails from Usupovo Life Hall

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Spam/Junk Folder">
    Search for emails from `tickets@rexcorp.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend Ticket">
    Go to `https://usupovo.rexcorp.com/my-tickets` and click "Resend".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If you still have issues, email `support@rexcorp.com` with purchase details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
